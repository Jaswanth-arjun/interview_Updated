Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

1. General Refund Information

Because Interview Copilot uses a prepaid, consumption-based credit model where wallet balance is spent in real-time to cover API processing costs, we generally do **not** offer refunds once credits are consumed.

2. Transaction Failures and Double Deductions

If you are charged for a credit package purchase but the wallet credits fail to load onto your account dashboard, please contact our support team immediately at: jaswantharjun20@gmail.com.

In cases of confirmed transaction failures or double billing, we will issue a full refund to your original payment method. The refund will be processed and credited back to your account within 5-7 business days as per standard banking procedures.

3. Cancellations

Since this is a consumption-based desktop helper utility with no recurring subscription fees, there is no recurring subscription to cancel. You can stop using the application at any time.
