Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy details how we handle information inside Interview Copilot.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to deliver the service safely:

  • Authentication Data: Email address, name, and profile avatar fetched securely via Google Sign-In.
  • Hardware Identity: Local motherboard UUID and network interface signatures to prevent multi-device free tier abuse.
  • Transaction logs: Credit wallet balances and billing ledgers connected to your account.

2. Data Usage

Your hardware identifiers are encrypted and kept strictly on our database to maintain account restrictions. Audio recordings used for transcription are processed dynamically and are **never stored** on our servers after transcription is finished.

3. Payment Processors

Payment information is processed securely through our payment partner, Razorpay. We do not store your credit card, netbanking credentials, or UPI details on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

Our service communicates with AI service APIs (like Gemini and Groq) to generate answers. Relevant question context is sent to these services anonymously to fulfill requests.
